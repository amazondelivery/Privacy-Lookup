TITLE: Facebook Privacy Settings
UPDATED: December 2023

DESKTOP:
STEP: Click the down arrow in the top right corner
STEP: Select "Settings & Privacy" then "Settings"
STEP: Click "Privacy" in the left sidebar
STEP: Adjust "Who can see your future posts"

MOBILE:
STEP: Tap the hamburger menu (three lines)
STEP: Scroll down and tap "Settings & Privacy"
STEP: Tap "Settings"
STEP: Tap "Privacy Settings"
